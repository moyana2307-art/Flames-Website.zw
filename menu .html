Our Story

Flames was founded with a simple goal: making food ordering easier and more accessible for everyone. We started with a passion for bold flavors and fast service, and we've been growing ever since.


From our first order in Victoria Falls to thousands of happy customers, every flame we spark is for you.

Mission

To provide fast, reliable, and affordable food & delivery services while supporting local restaurants and communities across Zimbabwe.

Vision

FAQs

Got questions? We've got answers.

How do I place an order?▼
Browse our menu, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Enter your delivery address and choose a payment method to confirm your order.
How long does delivery take?▼
Most orders are delivered within 30–45 minutes. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and order volume.
What payment methods do you accept?▼
We accept Ecocash, Visa, Mastercard, and PayPal payments.
Can I cancel my order?▼
You can cancel within 5 minutes of placing your order. Contact support for cancellations after that window.
Do you deliver outside Victoria Falls?▼
Currently we deliver within Victoria Falls only. We are expanding to other cities soon!

Refund Policy

We stand by every order.

Our Refund Guarantee

If there's an issue with your order, we'll make it right. Below are the conditions under which refunds are processed:

Wrong Item Received

Full refund or replacement if the item delivered does not match what was ordered.

Late Delivery

If your order arrives more than 60 minutes past the estimated time, you qualify for a partial refund or discount on your next order.

Quality Issues

If the food quality does not meet our standards, we will refund or replace the affected items.

Cancellation

Orders cancelled within 5 minutes of placement are fully refunded. Cancellations after that are handled case by case.

For refund requests, contact us at hello@flames.co.zw or call 📞 1800-FLAMES.
